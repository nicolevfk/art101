Pseudocode for Making Pasta from Scratch

  1. Get ingredients
    1. 2 cups of flour
    2. 2 large eggs
    3. 3 egg yolks
  2. Form pasta dough
    1. Make well with flour
    2. Add eggs to flour and mix with fork
    3. knead dough for 8-10 mins
    4. Let the dough rest for at least 30 minutes
  3. Roll out dough
    1. Using a pasta machine or rolling pin, roll the dough out lengthwise slightly
    2. On a lightly floured surface, fold the pasta dough in thirds
    3. Repeat these 2 steps until the pasta dough is smooth
    4. Now roll the dough out until it is around 1/16 inch thick
  4. Cut into pasta shape
    1. Cut pasta into sheets, around 12-14 inches long
    2. Dust both sides of the dough with flour
    3. After cutting to desired shape, stack pasta to the side and cover with a clean, dampened kitchen towel
